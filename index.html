About ATS Resume Builder

ATS Resume Builder is a modern, browser-based tool designed to help job seekers create professional, recruiter-ready resumes in minutes. The platform generates resumes that are fully compatible with Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), ensuring your application passes automated screenings and reaches hiring managers. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the tool allows you to enter your details step by step, preview your resume instantly, and download it in DOCX or PDF formats. Our goal is to simplify resume creation with a clean design, intuitive workflow, and data-driven structure that highlights your skills and experience effectively—helping you stand out in today’s competitive job market.
